Team Management Software

Team Management System is a practical software that serves the purpose of encapsulating workload of managing teams in an academic environment. In other words, it is a web application for team management which facilitates the creation of teams of students for projects/assignments/ presentations and also provides a mechanism for inter team management. The primary task of the system is to reduce the manual workload related to team management, entirely shifting to automation. Creating teams for a course has always been a puzzling challenge for professors. The goal of the Team Management System is to facilitate the creation of teams of students for projects, assignments, and other types of team works.Team Management, a system that serves the purpose of encapsulating workload of team management into a product. In other words, giving a new definition to the word “team management” that is reducing the manual, entirely shifting to automatic system.




Features

Team Management

We believe work becomes easy and exceptionally well if done in team. The load of making teams and selecting the basis is just a single click away.

Task Scheduling

Task allocation, scheduling has always been an issue while working in a team. Product allows you to focus on task, and it will schedule task for you and even make you remember about your duty towards it.

Task Management

Do you want to know if your team mate is done or still lagging behind the deadline. This features provide a lookup to what has been done and in what time

Meeting Scheduling

Team leader biggest challenge to schedule meeting and to notify each member about it, still people forget time. This Feature will notify you even just before the meeting and it handles minutes of meetings as well.

Customize Team Generation

Feature for professor, Criteria for team generation can be customized according to what the professor wants. Be it CPI ,Skills, or random generation.
